1. Viewer Help: Rentals

Issues with playback, 3-7 day window, receipts, or access? Email your order ID, film title, device, and a short description. Email is required at checkout to deliver your rental and receipts.

2. Viewer Help: Payments

Failed transaction, duplicate charge, or no access after payment? Include the email used for purchase. We do not store full card details. Refunds follow our Refund Policy.

3. TVOD Model

4th Ground is TVOD only. Rent individual films for 3-7 days. No subscriptions. All films are fully licensed, including music and artwork. "Coming soon" means license secured.

4. Filmmaker Help: Submissions

Rights holders: submit films via support@4thground.com Subject: "Film Submission". Include EPK, license docs, and payout email. We only accept fully cleared content.

5. Filmmaker Help: Payouts

Missing payout or statement? Email with your film title and registered payout email. Payouts require your email + banking/tax details. Data is stored separately per our Privacy Policy.

6. Technical Issues

DRM, login, app, or browser problems? Include device, OS, browser, screenshot, and time of issue. For account/POPIA requests, see Section 7.

7. Account & POPIA Requests

Access, correct, or delete your data under POPIA. DMCA notice or counter-notice? Email support@4thground.com with Subject: "POPIA Request" or "DMCA Notice". Response time: 48 hours for urgent access issues.
