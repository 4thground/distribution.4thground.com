Rentals

All content available on 4TH GROUND is distributed under a Transactional Video on Demand "TVOD" model. TVOD access is granted for a defined licensing window only and does not constitute a transfer of ownership of the underlying audiovisual work or any associated intellectual property rights. Rentals are made available for a viewing period of 3 to 7 days, commencing upon the earlier of 1) the start of playback or 2) 48 hours after the transaction is completed, unless a different period is specified on the title page prior to purchase. During the rental period, the title may be streamed an unlimited number of times on supported devices within the authorized territory. Purchases provide ongoing access to the licensed title for the duration that 4TH GROUND maintains streaming rights to such title and subject to continued availability of the platform and user account in good standing. All rentals and purchases are non-transferable, non-refundable, and for personal, non-commercial use only. Electronic receipts are issued automatically to the email address associated with the 4TH GROUND account upon successful completion of payment. Refunds or credits will only be considered in limited circumstances, including 1) duplicate charges due to a technical error on our platform, 2) failure to deliver access where no playback was initiated and the issue is confirmed to be on 4TH GROUND’s systems, or 3) where required by applicable consumer protection law in your jurisdiction. Requests for refunds must be submitted to support@4thground.com within 7 days of the transaction date, together with the order number and account details. 4TH GROUND reserves the right to deny refund requests where playback has commenced, the rental window has expired, or the issue results from user device, network, or third-party service failures. By completing a TVOD transaction, you acknowledge and agree to these licensing terms, viewing windows, and refund limitations.

Streaming & 4K Playback

4TH GROUND delivers TVOD content via adaptive bitrate streaming to optimize for 4K Ultra HD, Full HD, and SD quality based on your device capability and internet connection. To minimize playback issues and buffering, we recommend a stable minimum connection of 25 Mbps for 4K, 10 Mbps for Full HD, and 5 Mbps for SD streaming, preferably over a wired Ethernet connection or 5GHz Wi-Fi with low network congestion. If you experience buffering, stuttering, or quality downgrades, try closing background applications, restarting your router, reducing other devices on the same network, or manually selecting a lower resolution in the player settings. 4K playback requires HDCP 2.2 compliance, a 4K-capable display, and a supported device or browser. 4TH GROUND is supported on the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari, and Microsoft Edge on Windows and macOS; iOS 15+ via Safari; Android 10+ via Chrome; Apple TV 4K, Android TV/Google TV, Roku, Amazon Fire TV, and select Smart TVs. Use of outdated browsers, unsupported devices, VPN/proxy services, screen recording software, or network configurations that block DRM may result in playback errors, black screens, or inability to stream in HD/4K. 4TH GROUND utilizes studio-grade Digital Rights Management "DRM" to protect content, and playback is only permitted within the terms of your rental or purchase license. For technical assistance, email support@4thground.com with your device model, browser version, connection speed, and a description of the issue.

Account & Billing

Please note: 4TH GROUND does not require user accounts, sign-in, or profile creation. All rentals and purchases are processed as guest transactions through our secure payment partners. You do not need a password, username, or account to access content. For this reason, we are unable to assist with login issues, password resets, or account changes, as no accounts are maintained on the platform. Payment methods are limited to those accepted by our third-party checkout provider at the time of transaction, and all payments are processed off-site in compliance with PCI standards. An electronic invoice/receipt is sent automatically to the email address you provide during checkout. Please ensure this email is accurate, as it is the only record of your purchase and the method by which access links or support can be delivered. If you do not receive your receipt, check spam/junk folders or contact support@4thground.com with your transaction date, amount, and last 4 digits of the card used. Changes to payment methods, billing details, or invoices after checkout cannot be made, because transactions are final and no account profile exists to modify. Refund requests are handled per our TVOD Refund Policy only.
