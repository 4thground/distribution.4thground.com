About Us

Who We Are

4TH GROUND is a film distribution company founded and run by Independent film entrepreneurs who are passionate about bridging the gap between independent filmmakers and international audiences. We understand the challenges that independent producers face when trying to access professional distribution channels, and we are committed to building a more open, accessible, and filmmaker-focused pathway into the global streaming landscape.

Pre-Sale Movie Store

We operate our own TVOD platform where filmmakers can start earning revenue while waiting for their films to be picked up by major platforms. Filmmakers suggest their own rental and buy prices, keeping control of their work and connecting directly with audiences. No gatekeepers, just immediate access to viewers.

Global Presence

We are based in Johannesburg, South Africa. To ensure closer collaboration with filmmakers and strengthen our global reach, we also have an acquisitions team based in the United States and United Kingdom. This allows us to stay closely connected to key industry territories, engage directly with international partners, and provide filmmakers with more responsive, hands-on support throughout the distribution process.

Filmmaker-First Mission

We exist to champion untold stories. 4TH GROUND is filmmaker-focused and creativity-first. Every decision we make is about giving independent creators leverage, transparency, and access to global audiences without compromising their vision or ownership.
