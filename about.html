About Us

4ᵀᴴ GROUND is a film distribution company founded and run by independent film entrepreneurs who are passionate about bridging the gap between independent filmmakers and international audiences. We understand the challenges that independent producers face when trying to access professional distribution channels, and we are committed to building a more open, accessible, and filmmaker-focused pathway into the global streaming landscape.

We are based in Johannesburg, South Africa. To ensure closer collaboration with filmmakers and strengthen our global reach, we also have an acquisitions team based in the United States and United Kingdom. This allows us to stay closely connected to key industry territories, engage directly with international partners, and provide filmmakers with more responsive, hands-on support throughout the distribution process.
